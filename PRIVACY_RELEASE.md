# Privacy Release — Interview Recording

I, the undersigned, am participating in a recorded coding interview for
the role described in `INTERVIEW_RULES.md` of this repository.

## What is recorded

- **Terminal recording (`terminal.cast`)** — a text-based recording of
  everything visible in my terminal during the interview, captured by
  asciinema.
- **AI agent log (`interview.log`)** — a JSONL log of prompts I sent to
  my AI coding agent and the tool calls the agent made.
- **Git history** — all commits I make during the session.
- **Audio transcript** — from the video conference platform's transcription
  service.

## Our privacy commitment to you

- **We respect your privacy.** Stratus takes the privacy of every
  candidate seriously.
- **Limited use.** We will not use your interview data — your
  submission, recording, log, or any related materials — to inform
  anything outside of this interview process.
- **Confidentiality.** We are committed to not sharing your information
  with anyone outside the company.
- **Human review.** Anything you produce during the course of the
  interview will be reviewed by humans, and humans have final say in
  all hiring decisions.
